Character

Kaden: Identity: The Assassin sent by the kingdom of Venda to kill Princess Lia. Origin: He is from Venda, the savage kingdom that is considered an enemy to Morrighan. Skills: Highly trained assassin, expert in tracking, fighting, and camouflage. He is exceptionally stealthy and observant. Personality: Brooding, intense, cynical, and emotionally guarded, but fiercely loyal and protective of those he cares about. He possesses a dry wit that surfaces occasionally. Rafe: Identity: The Crown Prince (and later King) of Dalbreck. Origin: The Royal House of Dalbreck, the wealthiest and most militarily advanced of the three main kingdoms. Skills: Political savvy, excellent swordsman, adept strategist, and skilled at maintaining his cover (he posed as a common worker when seeking Lia). Personality: Commanding and regal, but also kind and brave. He can be stubborn, blunt, and highly protective of Lia, sometimes to the point of being controlling. Lia: Identity: Princess (First Daughter) of Morrighan, the kingdom of tradition and desert terrain. Origin: The Royal House of Morrighan Skills: Initially thought to be just a princess, she proves to be highly resilient, a quick learner in survival, observant, and a formidable leader in battle and politics. Personality: Headstrong, compassionate, stubborn, and deeply devoted to her few close friends. She is a natural leader who inspires loyalty.

Book Summary

Kiss of deception: Summary: Princess Arabella Celestine Idris Jezelia flees her wedding day to work at an inn. She goes by her alias Lia and falls in love with the prince that she was supposed to marry, Rafe, and the assassin trained to kill her, Kaden. Lia struggles to hide her identity as she keeps up her newfound duties in the inn. Themes: - Tradition - Duty vs. desire - Deception and secrets Heart of Betrayal: Summary: After being captured by Kaden, Lia finds ways to adapt to Venda. While Rafe discovers much his kingdom is despised there. Rafe and Lia make plans to escape and head towards Dalbreck. But the Komizar has other plans for Lia that interfere with their escape plans. Themes: - Betrayal - Self-discovery - Political intrigue Beauty of Darkness: Summary: After escaping Venda half alive, Rafe tenderly cares for Lia as they journey towards an outpost. Lia soon learns that Rafe has no intention on taking her back to her kingdom. She journey’s back on her own and learns the full truth of the war that is raging in Morrighan. Themes: - Leadership - Love vs. duty - Survival
